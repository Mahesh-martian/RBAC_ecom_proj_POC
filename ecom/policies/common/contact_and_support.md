# Contact and Support

This guide explains how to reach ShopEase support, whether you are a customer or a seller.

## Getting Help

- Most questions can be answered from your account dashboard, where you can view orders, returns, and account details.
- The ShopEase assistant can answer policy and support questions and, for customers, help you find products.

## Customer Support

- For order, delivery, return, refund, or payment questions, open the relevant order from your account and use the support option there.
- Have your order number ready so we can help you faster.

## Seller Support

- For listing, fee, payout, performance, or policy questions, use the support option in your seller dashboard.
- For restricted-category approvals, contact seller support before listing.

## Response Times

- We aim to respond to support requests within 2 business days.
- Urgent account-health or payment issues are prioritised.

## Account Security

- ShopEase will never ask for your password. If you receive a suspicious message, do not respond and report it through your dashboard.

# Privacy and Data

This notice explains, at a high level, how ShopEase handles personal data for everyone who uses the marketplace, including customers and sellers.

## What We Collect

- Account information you provide, such as name, email, and contact details.
- Transaction information needed to process orders and payouts.
- Usage information, such as pages viewed and actions taken, to operate and improve the service.

## How We Use Data

- To create and manage your account.
- To process orders, payments, and seller payouts.
- To provide customer and seller support.
- To detect and prevent fraud and abuse.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## Sharing

- We share order details between the relevant customer and seller only as needed to fulfil and support a purchase.
- We use trusted service providers (for example payment processors and carriers) under appropriate safeguards.
- We do not sell your personal data.

## Your Choices

- You can view and update your account information from your dashboard.
- You can request deletion of your account subject to completing open orders and meeting legal retention requirements.

## Security

- We use industry-standard safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest. No method is perfectly secure, so please keep your credentials confidential.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact ShopEase support through your account dashboard.

# Account and Customer Support Policy

Account access:
- Keep your password private and update it regularly.
- If you suspect unauthorized access, reset your password immediately.

Support channels:
- Use in-app support chat for product and policy questions.
- Provide order number for faster help on shipping, cancellation, or returns.

Response targets:
- Standard support requests are typically answered within 24 hours.
- High-priority order issues are prioritized for faster response.

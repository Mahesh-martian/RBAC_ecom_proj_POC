# Returns and Refunds Policy

You can request a return within 30 days from the delivery date.

Return conditions:
- Item must be unused and in original condition.
- Tags and original packaging must be present.
- Final-sale or clearance items are not eligible unless defective.

Refund timeline:
- After we receive and inspect the return, refunds are processed in 5 to 7 business days.
- Bank posting time may vary by payment provider.

Damaged or wrong item:
- Contact support with your order number and photos within 48 hours of delivery.
- We will arrange replacement or full refund based on the issue.

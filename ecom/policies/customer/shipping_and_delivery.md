# Shipping and Delivery Policy

Standard shipping is 3 to 7 business days.
Express shipping is 1 to 3 business days where available.

Shipping charges:
- Orders over USD 50 get free standard shipping.
- Orders below USD 50 have a flat shipping fee.

Order tracking:
- Tracking number is provided once your order is shipped.
- You can track shipment status in the Orders section.

Delays:
- Delivery dates are estimates and can be affected by weather or carrier disruption.
